Hi, I’m Johannes.

I am a designer based in Leipzig, Germany.
My work focuses on designing for the visual, auditory, and
haptic senses and I strive to create both meaningful and delightful experiences for people.

I’m part of the design team at ottonova.

In 2024, I graduated from Dessau Department of Design with a master’s degree in Intermedia Design.

Whilst I’m updating my portfolio, you can find some of my work on Instagram and Dribbble.
